Sawaal Jawab

Aksar pooche jaane wale Sawaal

Admission ke liye kya age limit hai? +
Koi age limit nahi. Play Group se le-kar adults tak — har koi admission le sakta hai. Har umar ke liye course available hai.
Kya mein ek saath do courses le sakta hoon? +
Bilkul! Aap School Education ke saath Computer Course ya English Language Course bhi le sakte hain. Timings adjust ki ja sakti hain.
Fees kya hai? +
Fees course aur class ke mutabiq mukhtalif hai. Barah-e-karam school aayein ya call karein — hum aapko mukammal fee details de denge.
Kya certificate milta hai? +
Haan, course mukammal karne par IBS ki taraf se certificate diya jata hai jo aapke skills ko officially recognize karata hai.
Shifts kaise hain? +
Morning shift subah 8 AM se 1 PM tak aur evening shift 3 PM se 7 PM tak hai. Aap apni sahulat ke mutabiq shift choose karein.
